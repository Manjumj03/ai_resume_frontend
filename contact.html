Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our AI Resume Builder

Getting Started

How does the AI optimization work?

Our AI analyzes job descriptions and suggests relevant keywords, phrases, and formatting to help your resume pass through ATS systems and catch recruiters' attention. It uses advanced natural language processing to match your skills with job requirements.

Do I need to create an account to use the service?

Yes, creating a free account allows us to save your progress, store multiple resume versions, and provide personalized recommendations. Your account also gives you access to additional templates and features.

Resume Creation & Editing

Can I edit my resume after creating it?

Absolutely! You can update and modify your resume anytime through your dashboard. Our platform saves your progress automatically, so you never lose your work. You can also create multiple versions for different job applications.

How many resume templates are available?

We offer 15+ professional templates designed for different industries and career levels. All templates are ATS-friendly and optimized for modern hiring practices. Premium users get access to exclusive designs and customization options.

What file formats can I download?

You can download your resume in PDF, Word (DOCX), and plain text formats. All formats are optimized for ATS compatibility. PDF is recommended for most applications, while DOCX is preferred by some companies for easy parsing.

Account & Billing

What's the difference between free and premium plans?

Free users can create 1 resume with basic templates and download in PDF format. Premium users get unlimited resumes, all templates, multiple file formats, AI-powered job matching, and priority support. Check our pricing page for detailed comparisons.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees or hidden charges.

Privacy & Security

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) and security measures to protect your personal information. Your resume data is stored securely and never shared with third parties without your explicit consent. We comply with GDPR and other privacy regulations.

Can I delete my account and data?

Absolutely. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your account settings. This action is permanent and cannot be undone. We also provide data export options before deletion.

Technical Support

What browsers are supported?

Our platform works best on modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend using the latest version for optimal performance. Mobile browsers are also supported for viewing and basic editing.

How fast is your support response time?

We typically respond to support requests within 24 hours during business days (Monday-Friday, 9AM-6PM EST). Premium users receive priority support with faster response times. Emergency issues are handled immediately.
